SELF-AWARENESS

SELF-AWARENESS

Knowing yourself is at the heart of one’s well-being and managing our emotions well. But the opportunities for acquiring it have been restricted. It’s taken too long, been too chancy or too costly. The current lead-vehicles for self-knowledge – books, internets – are highly inefficient, are directed at a generic reader rather than you in particular and carry insights that drain quickly from our leaky minds.

Nyx will draw on more reliable information about what is really going on inside your brains and will tell you and those you care about who you are, what you are feeling and what you should optimally seek at any point in time.

At present, we are often deeply ignorant about where our talents lie, what our flaws are and what are true interests might be. Our consciousness doesn’t grasp at all well what is happening to its owner. For example, I might not realize that the true cause of my irritability is tiredness; I might think it’s simply that my partner is being excessively obtuse. But NYX will get us the self-knowledge we need. It will map brain activity and alert us in good time as to the reality of our psychological lives.
